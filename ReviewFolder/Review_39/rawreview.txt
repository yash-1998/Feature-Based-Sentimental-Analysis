effortless class Only 3 mins stroll from the scrum of the
Rialto, here is another world, calm and still.
From the brass bell and massive wrought iron gate that open onto the small courtyard, through to all the details of the public areas and rooms, there is an impression of care and attention which must be down to the personal taste and presiding interest of the owners.
Don't expect fawning service or overblown breakfasts - this is a place for adults to be treated as such.
We usually check out other hotels in town for future reference - in Venice we just didn't bother.
